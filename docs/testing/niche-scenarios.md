# Niche Modeling Scenarios

List of high-value financial modeling niches where incumbents are weak. Track our progress in these realms.

## 1. Private Markets & Alternative Assets
- **LP/GP Waterfall Calculations**: Complex carry structures, catch-ups, clawbacks across multiple vintages
- **NAV Roll-Forward Models**: PE/VC valuation adjustments with cohort tracking and mark-to-market
- **Private Credit Covenant Testing**: Middle-market covenant packages with custom add-backs and carve-outs
- **Complex Fund Structures**: Master-feeder, parallel vehicles, sidecars with varied fee/promote arrangements

## 2. Specialized Regulatory & Compliance Modeling
- **IFRS 17 Insurance Contract Models**: CSM calculations, risk adjustments, discount rate curves
- **CECL/IFRS 9 Expected Credit Loss**: Probability-weighted scenarios, macro overlays, PD/LGD/EAD
- **Climate Risk Scenario Testing**: TCFD-style shock scenarios, forward stress-testing grids
- **Operational Resilience Models**: Interconnected impact analysis for operational risk and recovery planning

## 3. Cross-Border & Multi-Entity Complexities
- **Intercompany Eliminations**: Automated consolidation adjustments and elimination entries
- **Transfer Pricing Documentation**: Armâ€™s-length pricing with comp-benchmarks and safe-harbor logic
- **Multi-Currency Revaluation Engines**: Transaction vs translation FX impacts, MTM swaps, OCI flows
- **Tax Treaty Optimization**: Holding structures with mixed-jurisdiction tax rate tables and treaty benefits

## 4. Specialized Industry Models
- **Biotech R&D Portfolio Valuation**: Risk-adjusted NPV for drug pipelines, milestone probability curves
- **E&P Reserve Valuation**: Decline curve forecasting, royalty structures, unit-of-production depletion
- **Infrastructure Concessions**: PPP models with usage fees, CPI escalators, long-term forecasting
- **Insurance Loss Triangles**: Actuarial development patterns, claims lag, chain-ladder methods

## 5. Complex Deal Structures & Special Situations
- **SPAC Merger Modeling**: De-SPAC mechanics, earn-outs, sponsor promote, lockups
- **Restructuring & Distressed M&A**: DIP financing, 363 sales, creditor recovery waterfall analysis
- **Joint Venture Economics**: Uneven capital contributions, distribution waterfalls, exit triggers
- **Litigation Finance Models**: Case success probability, portfolio diversification, IRR optimization

## 6. Middle & Back-Office Automation
- **Treasury ALM Modeling**: Interest rate risk, liquidity gap analysis, projected cash flows
- **Risk Parameter Calibration**: PD/LGD/EAD statistical back-testing, scorecard integration
- **Capital Allocation (RAROC)**: Risk-weighted returns, economic capital, performance attribution
- **Stress Testing Frameworks**: Automated CCAR/EBA/IFRS 9 scenario generators and reporting templates
